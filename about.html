03 : About me

Hello! Nice to meet you :)

As you may already know, my name is Alexandre Manai.

I'm a 21 year old student from Paris pursuing my bachelor's degree in Computer Science at ETH Zürich.

My parents being french and german and going to an International School with over 19 different Nationalities,

I was fortunate to experience different perspectives which helped me understand the importance of community,

sharing ideas and mutual support.

I've been playing soccer for 11 years now. Being the captain of my team taught me responsibility and leadership.

I learned that being captain doesn't always mean seriousness and rigour, but flexibility and being understanding

towards everyone. Trying to see each person's point of view is key to bring everyone together towards a common goal.

In my spare time, I've also been shooting videos and editing pictures especially for the instagram account

@80cartoonz. Seeing all those creative ideas come together in one end result is always so fulfilling and rewarding.

But one thing that has always interested me is how nature works. Especially Evolution and Intelligence, in the broad term.

Scientific ideas and projects that turned to nature to find answers have always fascinated me. Artificial Intelligence

has been my biggest interest. Understanding different algorithms and implementing them is so much fun for me!
