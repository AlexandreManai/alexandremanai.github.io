005 : StudyLLM

- Language of implementation: Python

- Technology Stack: Python, OpenAI, Langchain, Streamlit

- Platform: Web-based Application

- Code:

StudyLLM is all about redefining how we interact with our study materials. Who would have thought that one day we could chat with our PDF notes or textbooks? That's right, StudyLLM isn't just an app; it's your personal study assistant. Whether you're drowning in notes or just need a quick recap, it's here to save your day!

And if you're someone who gets those pre-exam jitters, worry not! With its intelligent test prep feature, StudyLLM crafts a custom 10-question practice exam from your materials. It's an engaging, interactive way to ensure you're on top of your game!

Behind the scenes, the magic unfolds as our language model dives deep into your PDFs, transforming vast texts into digestible, actionable pieces. Simply pop a question and let StudyLLM retrieve the most relevant answer for you. It's language model wizardry at its finest!

As with all good things, sharing is caring. While this repository primarily serves educational purposes, the platform is an open invitation for all learners to adapt and tailor it to their needs. Dive deep, stay inquisitive, and remember: with StudyLLM by your side, acing those exams is just a chat away!
